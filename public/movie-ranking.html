Brandon's AniList

Brandon's top 3 anime series right now

MASHLE: MAGIC AND MUSCLES

It's about a guy that has no magic in a magic world, but overcomes the magic norm with hard working muscles.
He also has a cream puff addiction.

SPY x FAMILY Cour 2

It's the 2nd installment of Spy X Family, where 3 individuals come together and use each other as a "cover" under the guise of being a family.
The twist is that none of them really know the big secret each person is hiding except the mind-reading daughter named Anna.

Dr. STONE New World

It's about people who have been petrified by a mysterious beam of green light.
It's now the year 3000-whatever and one man emerges from the stone...Dr. Stone himself, Senku.
He begins his journey of science using whatever natural resources at his disposal to rebuild civilisation from the ground up using science.
